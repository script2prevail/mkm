Kho Kho Rules

🏃‍♂️

Team Composition

Each team consists of 12 players, with 9 taking the field and 3 substitutes.

⏱️

Time Limit

The match is played in two innings of 9 minutes each with a break in between.

➡️

Direction Change

Chasers can only change direction by touching a sitting teammate and shouting "Kho".

📏

Field Dimensions

The field measures 27m x 16m, divided into equal halves with a central lane.

🚫

Foul Play

Pushing, holding, or blocking runners is strictly prohibited and leads to penalties.

🏅

Winning Criteria

The team that tags the most opponents in the least time wins the match.
